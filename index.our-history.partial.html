OUR SHOP

Modern clothes should be comfortable, innovative and bright. Clothing is not just pieces of fabric. This is a way of self-expression. DIVISION is a perfect expression of the urban lifestyle.

In our collections, we were inspired by the streets of a big city. In our clothes, you will be noticeable in the crowd and feel comfortable in the gym. Each of our collections includes clothes, shoes, and accessories for women and men. You can purchase DIVISION items in our official offline store in New York, as well as on the sites of our partners.
